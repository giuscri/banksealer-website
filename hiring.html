Team

Alvise Biffi

CEO

Alvise graduated at Bocconi University and has been working in IT Security consultancy since 1999. He is CEO and co-founder of Banksealer and Secure Network. He is very active in the institutional context covering, among others, following roles: VP Piccola Industria at Confindustria; VP Piccola Industria Assolombarda (mandate for the CyberSecurity Advisory Board); Board Member for Italia Startup; part of the board of Confindustria’s Assinform. He also got several Public Awards for his commitment in social and economic development.

Stefano Zanero

Co-founder

Stefano received a Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from Politecnico di Milano, where he is currently an associate professor. His researches focus on malware analysis and cybersecurity: he co-authored over 60 scientific papers and books. He is a member of the Board of Governors of the IEEE Computer Society and a lifetime senior member of the ACM. Stefano co-founded the Italian chapter of ISSA (Information System Security Association) and sits in its International Board of Directors. Stefano is also co-founder of Secure Network.
