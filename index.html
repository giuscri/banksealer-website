Catch the fraud, the smart way

Banksealer is a Fintech company providing a semi-automatic decision support system to timely identify Internet banking frauds. We are a spin-off of three entities having concrete, specific experience in different contexts. We take advantage of their skills and know-how to offer a highly innovative fraud detection solution to financial world:

Politecnico di Milano is one of the most important engineering University in Italy and created the core of our software: the powerful algorithm based on advanced data mining and machine learning techniques.

Secure Network, a leading company in system security with headquarters in Milan and London, contributed to make the core of our software efficient in financial environment

Buildo is an innovative software company that optimized system’s solidity, making the software flexible, stable and fully accessible for every type of user.

Fraud detection made easy

Fraud detection in a smart and effective way

Banksealer is a tool capable of promptly detecting suspicious transactions made through Internet banking frameworks, improving prediction of potential frauds and, as a consequence, helping financial institutions to ensure higher security for their customers, together with better internal efficiency.

Intelligent
 core

Our software applies most advanced machine learning concepts, creating a behavioural schema of the user that reports those activities appearing appreciably different from his profile.

Decision-making support

First transactions are automatically listed according to the risk score that our system indicates, then we give the Analyst the possibility to further check those elements of the operation (Amount, Place, Timestamp etc.) that appear suspicious compared to user’s standard behaviour. This is why Banksealer is a concrete decision-making support, not a simple indicator based on unknown parameters.

Full protection

It doesn’t matter what type of activity needs specific analysis: bank transfers (performed even in mobile banking), phone recharges, cards activities, micro-payments or even only logins, our system is able to catch any suspicious behaviour!

Why Banksealer

The actual scenario

In Italy in 2016 more than 1 account holder on 2 used Internet banking. Mobile Banking (banking on smartphones, tablets etc.) increased of more than 80% compared to previous year. And Italy is still less “Internet literate” compared to other European countries, so there is still room for a huge increase. Simply: the bigger the number of users, the higher the risk of internet frauds

Protection for the money of your customers

Because of its nature, our software is not “threat-specific” and offers a wide protection shield from each type of Banking Trojan and phishing attack. Banksealer ensures +30% detection capability compared to traditional systems and up to 90% based on advanced fraud scenarios.

Ease and effectiveness of use

Our aim is not to increase the number of warnings but their quality so that Analysts can dedicate their time to really suspicious transactions only, instead of having too many “false positives”. Users behaviour can also be easily analysed and monitored thanks to our readable models.

Simple integration

Instead of the vast majority of competitors, Banksealer doesn’t force you to modify existing security system but makes it more performing thanks to its integration modules, optimizing costs and getting evident benefits for financial institutions.

Higher security

Banks are investing a lot to develop safer accesses to Internet banking frameworks (also following the recent PSD2 directive) but this is not enough. It is crucial to have high quality tools that are able to analyse users’ activities too, in order to have a higher security level and avoid economic or reputational damages to the institution.
